Highland Cattle

Calves from the top ranking cow are given higher social status. But all are fluffy.

  • Scientific Name: Bos taurus
  • Weigth: 500 (female) - 800 (male) kg
  • Heigth: 3.3 (female) - 4.0 (male) ft
  • Distribution: worldwide

Highland cattle are a Scottish cattle breed. Due to their native environment they are a robust breed with their long thick hair proceting them from weather and cold. Traditionally, Highland cattle were held as house cows. Despite the long horns, they are generally good-natured animals.
